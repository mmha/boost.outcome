Implementation of LEWG proposed expected<T, E>

expected<R> can hold a fixed variant list of empty, a type R or a lightweight std::error_code at a space cost of max(24, sizeof(R)+8). This corresponds to tribool::unknown, tribool::true_ and tribool::false_ respectively. This specialisation matches the proposed Expected implementation before standards. More...

This corresponds to tribool::unknown, tribool::true_ and tribool::false_ respectively. This specialisation matches the proposed Expected implementation before standards.
